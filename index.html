Health Tips

Maintaining a healthy BMI is important for overall well-being:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Stay physically active (at least 30 minutes daily)
  • Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours for adults)
  • Manage stress through meditation or hobbies
  • Stay hydrated (about 2 liters of water daily)

Note: BMI is a screening tool but doesn't diagnose body fatness or health. Athletes may have a high BMI due to muscle mass.

    Pomodoro Technique

    The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method:

    1. Choose a task to work on
    2. Set timer to 25 minutes ("Pomodoro")
    3. Work on the task until timer rings
    4. Take a 5-minute short break
    5. After 4 Pomodoros, take a 15-30 minute long break

    Tip: Customize the timer durations to match your attention span (e.g., 50/10 for some people).
